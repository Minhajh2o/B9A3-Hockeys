Clients Question

Answers to common inquiries about our services, programs, and offerings for a seamless experience with our hockey club
Reach out to our support team for personalized assistance

What types of hockey equipment do you provide

We offer a comprehensive range of top-quality hockey equipment, including sticks, skates, pads, and helmets, ensuring that players have access to the latest gear for optimal performance and safety.

What does your hockey training program entail?

Our hockey training program covers a spectrum of skills, from fundamental techniques to advanced strategies. We focus on enhancing agility, strength, and game intelligence to help players elevate their performance on the ice.

Do you offer private hockey lessons?

Yes, we provide personalized one-on-one coaching sessions with experienced trainers. Private lessons allow players to receive tailored guidance, addressing specific areas for improvement and accelerating their skill development.

Is ski touring available at your facility?

While our primary focus is on hockey, we do not offer ski touring. Our facility is dedicated to providing top-notch amenities and training for hockey enthusiasts.

How can I book a session at your hockey club?

Booking a session is easy! Simply navigate to our online booking platform, choose your preferred time slot, and complete the reservation. For any assistance, our customer support team is ready to help.

What is the pricing structure for your programs?

Our pricing varies based on the program selected. Please refer to our Pricing section for detailed information on the cost of equipment, training programs, private lessons, and any additional services. If you have specific inquiries, feel free to reach out to our team for clarification.
